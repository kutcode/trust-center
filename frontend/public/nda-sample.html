Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (Sample)

This is a sample NDA provided by Trust Center for demonstration and default project behavior. Replace this with your legal-approved NDA URL in Admin Settings for production use.

1. Purpose

The parties wish to evaluate a potential business relationship and may disclose confidential information for that purpose only.

2. Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes non-public technical, business, security, operational, and financial information, whether oral, written, digital, or visual.

3. Recipient Obligations

  • Use Confidential Information only for evaluation of the business relationship.
  • Protect Confidential Information with at least reasonable care.
  • Disclose only to personnel with a need to know and similar confidentiality obligations.
  • Do not reverse engineer or misuse materials provided.

4. Exclusions

Obligations do not apply to information that is publicly available, independently developed without use of disclosed information, or lawfully received from a third party without restriction.

5. Term

Confidentiality obligations apply from first disclosure and continue for five (5) years after the last disclosure, unless a longer period is required by law.

6. Return or Destruction

Upon written request, the recipient will return or destroy confidential materials, except where retention is required by law or backup policy.

7. No License

No intellectual property rights or other licenses are granted by disclosure.
